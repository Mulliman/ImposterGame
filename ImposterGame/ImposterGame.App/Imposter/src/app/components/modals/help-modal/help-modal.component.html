Viewing tips and tricks as...

Imposter Other Players
Remember, you are the Imposter! Remember, you are NOT the Imposter!
What to do

If you are NOT the imposter, your goal in this round is to try and convince your fellow players that you know what the secret word is without aiding the imposter.

The secret word is highlighted on your option grid. You now need to think of one word (and one word only) that will convince that you know what the secret word is.

However, don't be too obvious! Remember, the imposter does not know what the secret word is, and they will be trying to find it out!

You can all answer in any order you want. When you have thought of your word, submit it using the app and read it aloud to the other players.

If you are the imposter, your goal in this round is to try and convince your fellow players that you know what the secret word is.

You do not know what the secret word is, but every other player does. Using all the options on the grid and answers from other players, you now need to think of one word (and one word only) that you think will let the others know that you know what the secret word is.

The others are on the lookout for you, so you'll need to be cunning! You can answer at any time, do you wait for clues from all others first, or will this raise suspicion?

When you have thought of your word, submit it using the app and read it aloud to the other players.

 Tips and tricks


Answering earlier in a round is likely to prove that you don't need other answers to help you come up with a word.


Try not to choose an answer that is too specific, as this may give the imposter a valuable clue!


Going first is risky as you won't have any answers from other players to guide you, however going last may raise a lot of suspicion. Timing is key.


if you don't know the secret word, try and give answers that could apply to a few different options. Not too many though, as this could show you're bluffing.
What to do

If you are not the imposter, this is te time for you to interrogate other players if you think their answers are suspicious. If other users ask you to clarify your answer, make sure you don't give away any secrets, the imposter could be listening for clues.

When the discussion is over, you will need to accuse someone and make a wager based on how sure you are.

The more points you wager, the more points you get if you accuse the right person. Guess wrong, and the imposter will have the chance to get those points instead!

Also remember that you are part of a team; if the majority identify the imposter, everyone in the teams gets a bonus point.

If you are the imposter and you already know the secret word, this is your chance to blend in with the group and cast suspicion on other players. Ask questions to those you think gave the weakest answers.

If you are unsure on the secret word you need to use all your misdirection and subterfuge skills to evade capture and discover the secret word.

The other players will be on the hunt, and they will get points if they find you!

 Tips and tricks


If you are sure someone is not the imposter, try and phrase your justification in a way that they will understand but the imposter will not. They may vouch for you and the word will remain secret.


Look out for the person deflecting questions, and constantly checking the option grid.


The successful hunter targets the weakest in the group. This is the time to be ruthless.


Even if you think you may be caught, you can still get points for guessing the secret word so make sure you use this time well!
