Answer stage

What to do

If you are NOT the imposter, your goal in this round is to try and convince your fellow players that you know what the secret word is.

The secret word is highlighted in yellow on your option grid. You now need to think of one word (and one word only) that will convince that you know what the secret word is.

However, don't be too obvious! Remember, the imposter does not know what the secret word is, and they will be trying to find it out!

You can all answer in any order you want. When you have thought of your word, submit it using the app and read it aloud to the other players.

If you are the imposter, your goal in this round is to try and convince your fellow players that you know what the secret word is.

You do not know what the secret word is, but every other player does. Using all the options on the grid and answers from other players, you now need to think of one word (and one word only) that you think will let the others know that you know what the secret word is.

The others are on the lookout for you, so you'll need to be cunning! You can answer at any time, do you wait for clues from all others first, or will this raise suspicion?

When you have thought of your word, submit it using the app and read it aloud to the other players.

Tips and tricks

  • Answering earlier in a round is likely to prove that you don't need other answers to help you come up with a word.
  • Try not to choose an answer that is too specific, as this may give the imposter a valuable clue!
  • Going first is risky as you won't have any answers from other players to guide you, however going last may raise a lot of suspicion. Timing is key.
  • Try and give answers that could apply to a few different options. Not too many though, as this could show you're bluffing.
