Imposter!

Can you fit in the group despite not knowing what the rest of the group know?

Best played with 4 - 7 players.

Learn to play
Friends and family

Gather a crowd of budding spies and sleuths and ensure that you can all easily communicate.

Ideally play socially distanced in one room, but you can also play over video call!

 Secret words

At the start of each round, the host will select a category. This category contains a selection of words of which one will be selected to be the secret word.

Most players will see this secret word highlighted on their grid, however one player will not. This person is the imposter.

 Pick a word, any word

Once the round has started each player must say a word that relates to the secret word. As the imposter doesn't know what the secret word is, they must try to blend in with the answer that they choose.

Answers must be clear enough to prove that you are not the imposter, but you don't want to be so specific so that the imposter can guess what the secret word is.

Players can answer at any time. Answering early can reduce other's suspicion of you, do you want to risk being the last to answer?

 The interrogation

When all players have answered, players can then interrogate each other to try and determine who the imposter is without revealing the secret word.

The imposter needs to use this time to prove that they know what the answer is, without saying what it is. By deflecting accusations to other users can you avoid suspicion while also picking up clues to help you find the secret word?

 Accusations and wagers

After the interrogation has finished each player must then select the user that they think is the imposter.

If you are sure of your accusation, you can wager more points. If you are correct you will get all of those points added to your total.

However, if you guess wrong the imposter can win those points instead. Do you trust your gut?

 Consensuses and confessions

Once all wagers are placed, the votes will be tallied. As a collective, have you successfully found the imposter?

Once the imposter is revealed, they have a chance to guess the secret word.

If the imposter guesses correctly, your clues were too obvious and the imposter will get a point plus any incorrect wagers.

If the imposter does not guess the secret word, each player gets one bonus point each.
