Can you blend in with a group and discover the secret word, while avoiding suspicion from the other players?

Best played with 4 - 7 players.

Secret words

At the start of each round, the host will choose a grid. This grid contains 16 related words, one of which will be randomly selected as the secret word.

Most players will see this secret word highlighted on their grid, however one player will not. This person is The Imposter.
Pick a word, any word

Once the round has started, each player must say a word that relates to the secret word. As the imposter doesn't know what that secret word is, they must attempt to blend in with the answer that they choose.

Answers must be clear enough to prove that you are not the imposter, however you don't want to make it too easy for the imposter to guess what the secret word is!

Players can answer at any time. Hot tip - answering early can reduce other's suspicion of you. Do you want to risk being the last to answer?
The Interrogation

When all players have answered, it's time to interrogate each other to try and discover who the imposter is without revealing the secret word.

The imposter needs to use this time to prove that they know what the answer is, still without saying the secret word. By deflecting accusations to other users, can you avoid suspicion while also picking up clues to help you find the answer?
Accusations and Wagers

After the interrogation has concluded, each player must then select the user that they think is the imposter.

The more sure you are of your accusation, the more points you can wager. If you are correct, you will get all of those points added to your total.

However, if you guess wrong the imposter can win those points instead. Do you trust your detective skills?
Confessions and Conclusions

When all wagers are placed, the votes will be tallied. As a collective, have you successfully found the imposter?

Once the imposter is revealed, they still have a chance to guess the secret word.

If the imposter guesses correctly, your clues were too obvious and the imposter will get a point, plus any incorrect wagers.

If the imposter does not guess the secret word, each player gets one bonus point each.
